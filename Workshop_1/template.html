Universidad Distrital Francisco José de Caldas

About the University

Universidad Distrital Francisco José de Caldas is a public higher education institution located in Bogotá, Colombia. Founded in 1948, it is renowned for its academic excellence and commitment to research and social development.

Academic Programs

  • Engineering
  • Sciences and Education
  • Environment and Natural Resources
  • Technology
  • Arts

Mission

To educate critical and creative professionals capable of contributing to the sustainable development of Colombian society through education, research, and innovation.
